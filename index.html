The Malaysian Grand Prix: A Data Journey (1999-2017)

The Malaysian Grand Prix was a major international motorsport event held annually from 1999 to 2017. It took place at the Sepang International Circuit, a racetrack near Kuala Lumpur known for its unpredictable tropical weather and technical, high-speed turns.

This page uses interactive visualizations to help you explore the race's history - from the location of the circuit, to its race winners, fastest speeds, and team rivalries over nearly two decades.
